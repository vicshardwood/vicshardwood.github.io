SERVICES PROVIDED:

Hardwood Floor Installation:

We offer a variety of solid hardwood species. Choosing an unfinished solid hardwood will be installed and finished on site.
We also offer the installation of laminate, and pre-finished hardwood. The installation process involves the prep of existing area and installing your choice of new wood.


Hardwood floor refinishing:

The sand and finishing process includes sanding,staining and finishing of your hardwoods.
For complete sanding of your hardwood floors we offer dust containment which will reduce the amount of dust during the sanding process .
Our customers will choose stain samples from what we have to offer before the final sanding process.
Sanding/refinishing of existing hardwood- stain and finish options, repairs, water damage repairs, new baseboard installation, re-coating, removing and replacing existing baseboard, floor prep- removing existing floor- carpet, tile, linoleum, hardwood etc. plus trash removal.

Even with proper maintenance over time, eventually your hardwood floor may become dull or worn looking, It may be time for re-coating of your hardwood floor.
On average your hardwood flooring will need to be re-coated in 5-10 years from the installation.
